VIX•MARA offers a curated selection of pieces for the Home and Fashion.

We offer interior design, personal styling and event planning services in New England, Central and South Florida in the U.S.


Victoria Bevz

Originally from Odessa, Ukraine, Victoria is a fashion expert and avid chef. She is a graduate of Brewster Academy in Wolfboro, New Hampshire and holds a business degree from the University of Vermont.

Victoria has worked in finance for many years but her heart is in cuisine, fashion and vintage design. She is responsible for event planning and curating VIX•MARA’s vintage home pieces. Victoria offers personal chef services in Vermont and Florida.

She splits her time between Vermont and Palm Beach, Florida. Victoria travels around the world to find unique pieces for the home and for recipes she crafts using farm to table ingredients.

She is mother to a 9 year old fashionista whose favorite designer is Lilly Pulitzer. Victoria is passionate about dog rescue and her dog Lilly is a part of our family.


Marie Agostini

Marie was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico and grew up in Winter Park, Florida.

After studying in Spain and completing her post graduate studies in Rollins College in Winter Park, FL, Marie has lived and traveled around the world. She has developed a passion for art, interior design, architecture, and fashion.

She shares a beautiful home with Victoria’s brother and they split their time between New England and Florida.

Marie has special ties with Australia, where she has developed strong connections in the beauty and design industries.

Marie leads VIX•MARA’s image consulting services, business image development, modern and eclectic interior design.

VIX•MARA is the product of the minds of two women whose enthusiasm translates to the sophisticated style they share with the world. They have combined their tastes to create a luxury lifestyle brand you are sure to love.
