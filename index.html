VIX•MARA is Lifestyle Brand that offers a curated selection of pieces for the Home, Fashion and luxury Accessories

We're an International Retailer of Fine Home Goods, Fine Jewelry, Luxury Accessories, Modern and Vintage Houseware, High End Fashion, Australian and New Zealand made home goods, and Australian Designer brand Representative based out of New England, USA. We offer local personal stylist services in Hartford, Connecticut, Bergen County, New Jersey and New York City.

Victoria Bevz

Born in Odessa, Ukraine, Victoria is a fashion expert and avid chef. Victoria moved with her family to the United States when she was 7. She has had the privilege to attend Brewster Academy, a boarding school in the quaint New England town of Wolfeboro, NH followed by earning a degree in Business from the University of Vermont. While residing in Vermont, Victoria has worked in finance for many years but ultimately her passion is in fashion and vintage design. Victoria is responsible for curating the VIX MARA vintage home division. During her free time Victoria splits her time between Vermont and Palm Beach, Florida. Victoria has traveled around the world to find unique pieces for the home and for recipes she crafts using farm to table ingredients. Victoria is a mother to 9 year old Isabella who follows in her mother’s footsteps. Isabella (or Bella, as everyone knows her by) is a true fashionista whose favorite designer is Lilly Pulitzer and she aspires to be a designer or architect when she grows up. Victoria’s passions include animal rescue (her dog Lilly is a part of our family), as well as being a on the board of trustees on non-profit Angeles Para Honduras, which raises money for less-privileged children to attend school.

Marie Agostini

Marie was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico and grew up in Winter Park, Florida. Marie was fortunate to study abroad in Spain while earning her undergraduate degree from Rollins College in Winter Park, where she continued her post graduate studies. She has worked in the education field and non profit sector for most of her life. She continues to work in her family’s accounting practice with her father who is a CPA licensed in Florida, Pennsylvania and the commonwealth of Puerto Rico. Marie traveled around the world as a flight attendant with American Airlines and along with her parents, two nephews and their two beloved Maltese dogs. Because of her passion for diversity and other cultures, she has developed a special love for aesthetics, visual art, interior design, architecture, and fashion. Marie and Victoria’s brother share a beautiful lake home in Connecticut. They visit their families in Orlando and Palm Beach often and have traveled together around Florida, New England, the Caribbean, Europe and Asia. Marie also has special ties with Australia, where she has developed strong friendships and connections in the beauty, fashion and design industries. Marie is known for her love of children, animals, nature, health and fitness. Marie lead’s VIX MARA’s high fashion and interior design lines, focusing on mid century modern and modernist styles.

VIX MARA is the product of the minds of two women who’s enthusiasm translates to the sophisticated style they share with the world. They have combined their tastes to create a luxury lifestyle brand you are sure to love.
